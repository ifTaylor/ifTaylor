Areas of focus

Work that sits between software, machines, and the real conditions around them.

A lot of the interesting problems are not purely software problems and not purely hardware problems either. They show up in the handoff between systems, in field behavior, and in the details that do not matter until suddenly they matter a lot.

01

Software and controls

Application logic, interfaces, machine behavior, data flow, and the controls side of making a system predictable once it is actually running.

02

Robotics and field work

Integration, commissioning, edge cases, timing problems, and all the small realities that show up when a design leaves the desk and has to cooperate with hardware.

03

Radio and investigation

Antenna projects, measurement work, tuning, and the kind of hands-on experimenting that usually teaches more than the first pass through the theory.
